ABOUT

Innovate. Create. Inspire.

When innovation serves people, it changes what’s possible for students, educators, and communities.

At Impact Edvisors, we believe that education has the power to shape futures. Every project we take on starts from a simple idea: when innovation serves people, it changes what’s possible for students, educators, and communities.

We work alongside schools, organizations, and global partners to help educators design meaningful learning that prepares students for the world ahead. Our focus is on connecting strategy with practice—building the bridge from innovation to implementation.

Our work blends vision and practicality. We help leaders set direction, guide professional learning that strengthens educator capacity, and design student-centered programs that make innovation visible in classrooms. Across every level, we focus on one question: How does this create real, lasting impact for students and their futures?

Impact, for us, isn’t a metric or milestone. It’s a student discovering confidence in their own ideas. It’s a teacher rethinking what learning can look like. It’s a system finding ways to make progress sustainable. The landscape of education is shifting quickly, but this moment is full of possibility. With clarity, creativity, and collaboration, we can design the future together—one purposeful step at a time.

From Vision to Impact

We support the full journey of innovation, from big ideas to sustainable implementation. Our approach weaves three strands so leaders and educators have the clarity, tools, and models they need to make change that lasts.

Strategic Planning

Define goals and direction, align initiatives, and create pathways where innovation serves student learning and community priorities.

Professional Learning

Strengthen educator and leader capacity with workflows, examples, and coaching that move ideas into daily practice.

Innovative Program Design

Create real-world models of learning that make change visible and repeatable, with impact as a through-line from start to scale.

About Jeremiah Okal-Frink, Ph.D.

Jeremiah founded Impact Edvisors to help educators see what’s possible when innovation serves people. He partners with education systems, nonprofits, and global organizations to turn bold ideas into practical strategies. His work spans professional learning, innovation strategy, and system design, always grounded in one goal: to create lasting impact for students and their futures.
